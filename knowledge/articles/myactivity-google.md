Google My Activity is a privacy tool that allows users to view, manage, and delete their activity data across Google products. This includes search history, location data, YouTube watch history, and other Google service interactions.

## Key Takeaways

- Access your activity data at myactivity.google.com
- Use the "Delete" feature to remove specific time periods or all data
- Filter by Google product (Maps, Search, YouTube, etc.) for targeted deletion
- Choose time periods: last hour, last day, all time, or custom range
- This helps maintain privacy and control over your digital footprint
